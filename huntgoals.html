A lion’s mentality is all about focus, determination, and fearlessness. Lions are natural hunters, and they know what they want. They set their sights on their prey and pursue it relentlessly until they catch it. They don’t let obstacles or setbacks deter them from their mission.

  1. Turn goals into Prey
    • Start today and let the hunt begin
    • With a lion’s mentality and a clear plan of attack, you can achieve anything you set your sights on
  2. Steps to a Good Goal
    • Identify your prey – your ultimate goal
    • Develop a plan of attack
    • Break down your goal into smaller, achievable steps
    • Take action-Make proactive steps towards your goal every day
    • Stay focused on the end result and don’t let distractions or setbacks throw you off course
  3. How can we have the Lion’s mentality?
    • Mental Clarity

      • Form a clear and concise vision of what you want and who you want to be. Include all details.

    • Know it’s Yours

      • Envision it as already true. You already are who you want to be. You already have what you want.

    • Ignore Everything Else

      • You no longer waste the time or mental reserve for anything that isn’t contributing to your vision. No distractions.
